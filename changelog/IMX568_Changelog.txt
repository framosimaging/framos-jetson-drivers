v1.5.0.0 (2024-08-16)
=========================
    - added - support for L4T-36.3
    - added - detecting whether the connected sensor is monochrome or color
    - fixed - white lines when sensor is in slave mode
    - changed - support only for AGX Orin & Orin Nano/NX platforms
    - changed - support only for FPA-4.A/TXA, FPA-4.A/AGX & FPA-A/P22
